## Cultural Context (Muktadha wa Utamaduni)

### Politeness and Respect

- **Shikamoo** is essential when greeting elders or authority figures
- Always use polite forms with strangers: "tafadhali," "samahani"
- **Pole** (sympathy) is used constantly: for someone working, sick, or carrying something
- Use right hand for giving/receiving items (left hand is for hygiene)

### Proverbs (Methali)

| Methali | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Haraka haraka haina baraka | Haste has no blessings |
| Asiyefunzwa na mamae hufunzwa na ulimwengu | Life teaches what parents don't |
| Kidole kimoja hakivunji chawa | Teamwork over individualism |
| Mwacha mila ni mtumwa | Don't abandon your culture |
| Samaki mkunje angali mbichi | Discipline early |

### Addressing People

| Title | Usage |
|-------|-------|
| Bwana | Mr. |
| Mama | Mrs./Madam |
| Mzee | Elder, respected person |
| Mwalimu | Teacher (respectful) |
| Dada | Sister / female peer |
| Kaka | Brother / male peer |

### Time

Swahili time shifts by **+6 hours** from standard time:
- Saa moja asubuhi = 7:00 AM (not 1:00 AM)
- Saa sita mchana = 12:00 PM (noon)
